Section 1: Overview

Originator:Minnesota Department of Health

Title: Hospitals Serving Minnesota, 2020

Abstract: This data set is the result of an effort to identify hospitals both licensed to operate in Minnesota or significant to Minnesota citizens. For example, major hospitals in Fargo, North Dakota and La Crosse, Wisconsin are included due to their proximity to population centers in Minnesota that are closer to those facilities than other hospitals in Minnesota.

The main source of licensed hospitals is the Directory of Licensed and Certified Health Care Providers at the Minnesota Department of Health: https://gisdata.mn.gov/dataset/health-facility-providers

Additional non-Minnesota locations were manually sourced from open directories and public map databases.

Purpose: To provide state agencies and the general public with a basic overview of the locations of major hospitals that serve the general population, and provide links back to the Minnesota Department of Health licensing systems for pertinent details.

Lineage: Processing steps completed by Mike Dolbow, Minnesota Department of Education:

1. Downloaded from Minnesota Department of Health database - chose Hospitals, all counties, all types: https://gisdata.mn.gov/dataset/health-facility-providers
2. Removed header, deleted non-standard county columns, deleted empty columns
3. Geocoded with Esri World Geocoding Service
4. Manually reviewed against imagery, Google Maps, and Open Street Map imports
5. Moved point to building when warranted by manual review
6. Deactivated columns best sourced from MDH licensing information
7. Added several records "just across the border" using various sources. Assigned negative HFID numbers and filled in available information.
